Bad Apple ASCII Recreation

Project Overview

As a Touhou fan, also a enjoyer of The Touhou Project---A cluster of games and Nerds---I must show my support for MY game. Thus, I developed a ASCII based recreation of the iconic "Bad Apple" music video using Python libraries such as: Pillow to trace the video frames, Pygame to output music AND the video by frame.

Technical Implementation

  • Video frame extraction based on value and ascii
  • Real-time ASCII character mapping
  • Audio synchronization
  • Performance optimization for thousands of characters per frame
  • Originally a console application but expanded to a graphical interface for many reasons
